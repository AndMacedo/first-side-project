About Us

Colchis is a fabulously wealthy land situated on the mysterious periphery of the heroic world.

Colchis is located on the eastern shore of the Black Sea.

Colchis is really rich in gold, iron, timber and honey.

Colchis is known in Greek mythology as the destination of the Argonauts, as well as the home to Medea and the Golden Fleece.
